MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
Financial Services Confidentiality Agreement

Date: _________________

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into between:

(1) Global Financial Partners Inc., a corporation incorporated under the laws of New York, with registered office at 100 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005 ("GFP"); and

(2) ______________________, a _____________ incorporated under the laws of _____________, with registered office at ______________________ ("Counterparty").

Each of GFP and Counterparty is referred to as a "Party" and together as the "Parties".

RECITALS:
(A) The Parties are considering entering into discussions relating to potential financial services collaboration, investment opportunities, and/or strategic partnerships (the "Project").
(B) In connection with the Project, each Party may disclose Confidential Information to the other Party.
(C) The Parties wish to ensure that all Confidential Information disclosed is kept confidential.

THE PARTIES AGREE AS FOLLOWS:

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION
1.1 In this Agreement:
    "Confidential Information" means all information relating to a Party or its Affiliates which is disclosed by that Party to the other Party in connection with the Project, including:
    (a) Financial statements, projections, and analyses
    (b) Investment strategies and portfolio information
    (c) Client lists and account information
    (d) Proprietary trading algorithms and models
    (e) Risk management procedures
    (f) Regulatory and compliance information
    (g) Any analyses, compilations, studies or other documents prepared by the receiving Party containing or based on any of the above

    "Affiliate" means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a Party.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY UNDERTAKINGS
2.1 Each Party undertakes to the other that it will:
    (a) keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;
    (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any person except as permitted by this Agreement;
    (c) not use Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Project;
    (d) protect Confidential Information with security measures no less stringent than those it uses for its own confidential financial information;
    (e) comply with all applicable financial regulations regarding information security.

3. EXCEPTIONS
3.1 The obligations in Clause 2 do not apply to Confidential Information which:
    (a) is or becomes publicly available other than by breach of this Agreement;
    (b) the receiving Party can demonstrate was already known to it at the time of disclosure;
    (c) is received from a third party who lawfully acquired it and who is under no obligation restricting its disclosure;
    (d) is independently developed without access to or use of the Confidential Information; or
    (e) is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or regulatory authority.

4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURE
4.1 A Party may disclose Confidential Information to:
    (a) its directors, officers, employees, and professional advisers who need to know for the Project;
    (b) its Affiliates and their directors, officers, and employees who need to know for the Project;
    (c) any regulatory authority or governmental body having jurisdiction over it.

4.2 Before disclosure under Clause 4.1, the disclosing Party must ensure recipients are bound by confidentiality obligations no less onerous than those in this Agreement.

5. SECURITIES LAW COMPLIANCE
5.1 Each Party acknowledges that:
    (a) Confidential Information may constitute material non-public information under securities laws;
    (b) trading while in possession of such information may violate securities laws;
    (c) it will comply with all applicable securities laws and regulations.

6. TERM
6.1 This Agreement commences on the date of last signature and continues for 2 years.
6.2 The obligations under Clause 2 survive termination and continue for 5 years from disclosure of the relevant Confidential Information.

7. RETURN OF INFORMATION
7.1 On termination or on request, each Party must:
    (a) return all documents and materials containing Confidential Information;
    (b) permanently delete all electronic copies;
    (c) certify in writing compliance with this Clause 7.

8. NO OBLIGATION
8.1 Nothing in this Agreement obligates either Party to:
    (a) disclose any particular information;
    (b) enter into any further agreement; or
    (c) proceed with the Project.

9. REMEDIES
9.1 Each Party acknowledges that damages may not be an adequate remedy for breach of this Agreement and that the other Party may be entitled to seek injunctive relief.

10. GENERAL
10.1 Governing Law: This Agreement is governed by New York law.
10.2 Jurisdiction: The courts of New York have exclusive jurisdiction.
10.3 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties relating to its subject matter.
10.4 Amendment: This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both Parties.
10.5 No Assignment: Neither Party may assign its rights or obligations without the other Party's prior written consent.
